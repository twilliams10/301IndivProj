Sour Patch Watermelon


Sour Patch Watermelon are close relatives to the original Sour Patch Kids, the only exception is that they are colored and flavored to resemble watermelon. The Sour Patch brand belongs to Cadbury and the Malaco Licorice Company of Sweden. I like this candy because the flavor seems true to watermelon as an artificial flavoring and you won't have to worry about dividing up the flavors if you have to share with others.

Sour Patch Watermelon candies are also good to have if you add it to your favorite drink or your water to add a little flavor. Whether you get a big bag, or a more personalized bag, you wont have to worry about grabbing a certain flavor with the watermelon candy.
