Dots


With Dots, like other fruit candy, the fun is sticking your hand in and getting a flavor surprise in return. Dots have a solid array of flavors like orange, lemon, lime, strawberry, and cherry. Dots is owned by Tootsie Roll Industries. Dots are great sweet treat that really fill you up from their huge individual piece size. I find that I usually can't eat very many pieces at at time.

Dots are a great chewy, gummy candy that can also be eaten especially suring holiday seasons since Dots resemble gumdrops. Dots, during the winter holiday season, can also be used to decorate gingerbread houses for an extra pop of color.
